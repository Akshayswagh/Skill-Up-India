Admissions Information

Application Process

  1. Check your eligibility based on program requirements.
  2. Visit our admissions portal to apply online.
  3. Fill in your personal, academic, and contact details.
  4. Upload scanned copies of necessary documents.
  5. Submit the form and track your application status.

Eligibility Criteria

  1. Completion of 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board.
  2. Minimum 50% aggregate marks in qualifying exams.
  3. Relevant undergraduate degree for postgraduate programs.
  4. Valid entrance exam score (for selected courses).
  5. English proficiency test for international applicants.
Fee Structure & Admissions FAQs

Fee Structure & Scholarships

  1. Application Fee: ₹500 (non-refundable)
  2. Tuition Fee: ₹60,000 – ₹1,50,000/year (varies by course)
  3. Registration Fee: ₹5,000 (one-time)
  4. Scholarships:
    1. Merit-based scholarships up to 50%
    2. Need-based financial assistance available
    3. Early bird discounts for selected programs
