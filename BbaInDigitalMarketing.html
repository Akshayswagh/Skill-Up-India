B.B.A. Degree in Digital Marketing and E-Commerce

Why Choose a Degree in Digital Marketing and E-Commerce?

The digital revolution has transformed how businesses operate, making Digital Marketing and E-Commerce critical for success. With global online retail growing exponentially, businesses seek professionals skilled in SEO, social media, paid advertising, data analytics, and e-commerce management.

This B.B.A. in Digital Marketing & E-Commerce, aligned with NEP 2020, provides a multidisciplinary, hands-on learning experience, incorporating the latest industry trends, AI-driven marketing strategies, and real-world case studies to prepare students for exciting career opportunities in digital business management.

What Will You Learn in the Program?

  • Fundamentals of Digital Marketing & E-Commerce Business Models
  • SEO, Social Media Marketing, Google Ads & Performance Marketing
  • Data-Driven Marketing, AI in Marketing & Web Analytics
  • E-Commerce Store Setup, Operations & Conversion Optimization
  • Brand Management, Influencer Marketing & Consumer Behavior
  • Hands-on Industry Projects, Internships & Certifications

Curriculum Overview

The B.B.A. in Digital Marketing & E-Commerce follows the Choice-Based Credit System (CBCS) under NEP 2020 with a practical, application-based approach.

  • Total Credits per Semester: 20
  • Subjects per Semester: 5 (100 marks each)
  • Project/Internship per Semester: 1 (200 marks)

Each semester integrates core business subjects, specialized digital marketing & e-commerce courses, hands-on projects, and internships to develop industry-ready professionals.
