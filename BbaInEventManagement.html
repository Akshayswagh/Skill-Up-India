B.B.A. Degree in Event Management

Why Choose a Degree in Event Management?

The event management industry is booming globally, with businesses, brands, and organizations relying on professionally managed events for marketing, networking, and engagement. From corporate conferences, weddings, concerts, and sports events to festivals, brand activations, and influencer meetups, event management offers diverse career opportunities.

This B.B.A. in Event Management, aligned with NEP 2020, combines business acumen, creativity, logistics, and technology to prepare students for careers in event planning, brand management, hospitality, and experiential marketing.

What Will You Learn in the Program?

  • Fundamentals of Event Planning, Logistics & Operations
  • Marketing, Sponsorship & Brand Collaborations for Events
  • Technology in Events: Virtual, Hybrid & AI-Driven Events
  • Legal, Financial & Risk Management in Events
  • Production, Stage Management & Vendor Coordination
  • Hands-on Industry Projects, Live Events & Internships

Curriculum Overview

The B.B.A. in Event Management follows the Choice-Based Credit System (CBCS) under NEP 2020 with a practical, application-based approach.

  • Total Credits per Semester: 20
  • Subjects per Semester: 5 (100 marks each)
  • Project/Internship per Semester: 1 (200 marks)

Each semester integrates core business subjects, specialized event management courses, hands-on projects, and internships to develop industry-ready professionals.
