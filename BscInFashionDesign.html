B.Sc. Degree in Fashion Design

Why Choose a Degree in Fashion Design?

Fashion is a dynamic and ever-evolving industry that blends creativity, technology, and business acumen. A B.Sc. in Fashion Design provides students with technical expertise, industry exposure, and practical skills to thrive in the competitive world of fashion.

Aligned with NEP 2020 guidelines, this program integrates multidisciplinary learning, experiential education, and industry-relevant skills. It incorporates fashion technology, sustainability, digital fashion, and entrepreneurship to prepare students for diverse career opportunities in the global fashion industry.

What Will You Learn in the Program?

  • Fundamentals of Fashion Design & Textile Science
  • Illustration, Pattern Making & Garment Construction
  • Digital Fashion, CAD & Emerging Technologies
  • Sustainable Fashion & Ethical Design Practices
  • Fashion Business, Merchandising & Entrepreneurship
  • Hands-on Industry Projects, Internships & Portfolio Development

Curriculum Overview

The B.Sc. in Fashion Design is a three-year undergraduate program with a choice-based credit system (CBCS) as per NEP 2020.

  • Total Credits per Semester: 20
  • Subjects per Semester: 5 (100 marks each)
  • Project/Internship per Semester: 1 (200 marks)

Each semester combines core subjects, practical training, and industry projects to ensure holistic skill development.
