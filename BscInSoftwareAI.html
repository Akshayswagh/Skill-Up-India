B.Sc. Degree in Software Development – AI Specialization

Why Choose a Degree in Software Development – AI Specialization?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is reshaping industries by enabling automation, enhancing decision-making, and revolutionizing human-computer interactions. A B.Sc. in Software Development – AI Specialization prepares students to develop intelligent applications, implement machine learning models, and build scalable AI-driven solutions for sectors such as healthcare, finance, robotics, cyber security, and more.

Aligned with NEP 2020, this program integrates multidisciplinary learning, hands-on coding, industry projects, and AI-driven innovations, ensuring students graduate with cutting-edge technical skills and industry readiness.

What Will You Learn in the Program?

  • Core Software Development Principles & Programming
  • Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning & Deep Learning
  • Big Data Analytics, Cloud Computing & DevOps
  • AI-Powered Web & Mobile Application Development
  • AI Ethics, Responsible AI & Cyber security
  • Industry Projects, Internships & Research in AI

Curriculum Overview

The B.Sc. in Software Development – AI Specialization is a three-year undergraduate program with a choice-based credit system (CBCS) as per NEP 2020.

  • Total Credits per Semester: 20
  • Subjects per Semester: 5 (100 marks each)
  • Project/Internship per Semester: 1 (200 marks)

Each semester blends theoretical foundations, hands-on programming, and real-world AI applications to develop highly skilled AI software developers.
